As a company built on a foundation of teamwork, we see every project as an opportunity to partner with clients and produce top-notch results.

ERS has been providing content-driven, engaging, innovative, state-of-the-art programs for more than 23 years in virtually every therapeutic area.

1994

Gregg Terry launches Educational Resource Systems, Inc. (ERS), offering the pharmaceutical industry a comprehensive approach to sales training.

1995

ERS expands training solutions to include computer-based programs.

1999

Gregg Terry is nominated to the Juvenile Diabetes Resarch Foundation (JDRF) Board of Directors.

2000

ERS relocates to the Galleria in Red Bank, NJ, having outgrown its original offices in Shrewsbury, NJ.

2004

ERS expands its Sales and Marketing division.

2007

Johnson and Johnson, Inc. awards ERS with its highest level of information security.

2007

ERS establishes its Medical Communications Division.

2010

Apple launches the iPad®. ERS introduces iPad eLearning invitiative; staff programmers certified to develop iPad eLearning.

2011

ERS presents capabilities at Society of Pharmaceutical and Biotech Trainers (SPBT) national conference in Orlando, Florida.

2012

ERS developers are chosen as beta testers for Articulate Storyline, a revolutionary eLearning software that can be utilized via the iPad or PC with a single publish.

2013

ERS established its Medical Device Division.

2014

ERS expands its use of augmented reality beyond live meetings and into the distance learning training space.

2015

Innovation Finalist for LTEN Excellence Award.

2015

CiME, Consultants in Medical Education is Founded. MedScape partnership for CiME. Number of viewers of the program in record time.

2016

Innovation Finalist for LTEN Excellence Award.

2016

ERS develops our first virtual room eLearning tool for Stelara.
